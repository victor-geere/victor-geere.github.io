Critical Constructs

Construct

Greedy Harmonic Decomposition (GHD)

Description: Deterministic greedy sieve on [0, π] that decomposes any angle θ/π into a sum of harmonic step sizes αₙ = π/(n+2) using a Sylvester-sequence-inspired rule.

\[ \theta_{n+1} = \theta_n - \delta_n(\theta) \cdot \alpha_n, \quad \theta_0 = \theta, \quad \alpha_n = \frac{\pi}{n+2} \]

Reference: greedy-harmonic-decomposition.html

Construct

Indicator Function δₙ(θ)

Description: Binary indicator recording whether step n is accepted in the greedy decomposition of θ.

\[ \delta_n(\theta) = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{if } \theta_n \ge \alpha_n, \\ 0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} \]

Reference: greedy-harmonic-decomposition.html & greedy-harmonic-thresholds.html

Construct

Centred Functions φₙ(θ)

Description: Zero-mean versions of the indicator functions (deviation from uniform density θ/π).

\[ \phi_n(\theta) = \delta_n(\theta) - \frac{\theta}{\pi} \]

Reference: correlation-kernel.html

Construct

Correlation Kernel K(n,m)

Description: Positive semi-definite kernel encoding correlations between greedy digits. Core object of the spectral model.

\[ K(n,m) = \int_0^\pi \phi_n(\theta) \phi_m(\theta) \, d\theta \]

Reference: correlation-kernel.html & closed-form-kernel.html

Construct

Weighted Mellin Operator M_{n,m}(s)

Description: Kernel weighted by harmonic factors; its eigenvalues show massive suppression exactly at non-trivial zeros of ζ(s).

\[ M_{n,m}(s) = K(n,m) \cdot (n+2)^{-(s+1/2)} (m+2)^{-(s+1/2)} \]

Reference: greedy-harmonic-decomposition.html
