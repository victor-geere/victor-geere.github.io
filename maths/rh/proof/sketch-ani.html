Computation details (rigorous with respect to Conjecture 1):
Coefficients are given by \[ c_j = \sum_{p\le\lambda^2} \frac{\log p}{\sqrt{p}}\exp\!\Bigl(-i\frac{2\pi}{L}(j-N+\tfrac12)\log p\Bigr). \] The Newton polygon is the lower convex hull of the points \((j, \varphi_N(j/(2N)))\). The 3D helix visualises the Tropical Helix Regularisation \(\Psi(w)=(w,w^{1/(2\pi)})\); all roots lie on this helix when the polygon flattens.
