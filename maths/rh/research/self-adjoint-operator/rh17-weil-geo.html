2. Twin Conical Helices and the Prime Distribution

For \(\sigma > 1\), \[ \mathcal{C}_\pm(x) = \bigl( x^{-\sigma} \cos(t \log x),\; \pm x^{-\sigma} \sin(t \log x),\; x \bigr), \quad x \ge 1. \] Möbius inversion isolates \[ \tau_{\mathrm{prime}}(t) = \sum_{p,m \ge 1} \frac{\log p}{p^{m/2}} \delta(t - m \log p). \] Key Finding 1 (Unconditional): \(\tau_{\mathrm{prime}}(t)\) is derived solely from the Dirichlet series and Möbius function. Significance: This is the arithmetic side of the torsion identity and the classical shadow of the prime-sum side of the Weil explicit formula.
