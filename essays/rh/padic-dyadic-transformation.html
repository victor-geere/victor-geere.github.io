Introduction

p-adic sheaf cohomology (e.g., rigid cohomology, overconvergent F-isocrystals) has revolutionized arithmetic geometry but has not yet yielded a proof of the Riemann Hypothesis (RH). The dyadic sheaf cohomology (a speculative construction) aims to provide a binary-scaled analogue. Here, we construct a conjectural transformation equating the two, allowing p-adic tools to apply to dyadic settings for RH.

No such transformation exists in the literature; this is a novel proposal going beyond known solutions.

1. Current State of p-adic Sheaf Cohomology
