Riemann Xi Function with Substituted Integrals

The Riemann Xi function \(\xi(s)\) with \(f(x, s)\) and \(g(x, s)\) substituted by their definitions is given by:

\[ \xi(s) = \frac{1}{2} s (s - 1) \pi^{-s/2} \Gamma\left( \frac{s}{2} \right) \cdot \frac{1}{\Gamma(s)} \left( \int_0^\infty \frac{1}{2} \left[ (a + b i) \frac{x^{s - 1}}{e^x - 1} + (a - b i) \frac{x^{\overline{s} - 1}}{e^x - 1} \right] \, dx + i \int_0^\infty \frac{1}{2} \left[ (b - a i) \frac{x^{s - 1}}{e^x - 1} + (b + a i) \frac{x^{\overline{s} - 1}}{e^x - 1} \right] \, dx \right) \]
